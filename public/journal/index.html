The Micro Center at Crescent Hills

Jan 12

If you’re near a city, Micro Centers are good places to find computer parts. I’ve been eyeing this place on RR patrols for awhile. Looks to have been totally abandoned. No signs of vandalism or destruction. Here I was able to acquire a Raspberry Pi 5, monitor, keyboard, and a 32GB micro SD card.
