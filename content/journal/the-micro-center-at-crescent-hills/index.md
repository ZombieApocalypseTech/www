Jan 12

If you're near a city, Micro Centers are good places to find computer parts. I've been eyeing this place on RR patrols for a while. Tucked back in the Crescent Hills shopping center off Highway 57, seventeen miles due west of Heron Outpost, RU-20. Looks to have been totally forgotten. No signs of vandalism or destruction. Full inventory. Here I was able to acquire a Raspberry Pi 5, monitor, keyboard, and a 32GB microSD card. All brand new.
