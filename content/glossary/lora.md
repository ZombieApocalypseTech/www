**LoRa** (Long Range) - A low-power, long-range radio communication protocol ideal for off-grid messaging. [ZAC](/glossary/zac) uses LoRa via [Meshtastic](/glossary/meshtastic)-compatible devices to form mesh networks that can relay messages across kilometers without cellular or internet infrastructure.
