**The Turning** - Refers to the worldwide phenomena of human beings changing into something not quite human. This ongoing transformation has been responsible for the utter collapse of the social infrastructure. To date, there is no real understanding for the root cause of this phenomenon, though many are working to discover a cure. See also, [Zombie](/glossary/zombie).
