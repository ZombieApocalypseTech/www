Zombie Apocalypse Computer aka Zero-Infrastructure Autonomous Computer

A resilient, off-grid computing system designed to survive and operate during catastrophic infrastructure collapse. Built with longevity, repairability, and energy independence as core principles.

ZAC runs [ZACOS](/glossary/zacos).
